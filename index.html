Relevant Experience

Over the years, I have built a strong foundation in financial analysis and management, particularly in the field of accounting. As an **analyst** in various roles, I was responsible for processing, managing, and interpreting financial data to generate reports that helped guide strategic business decisions. My expertise in **accounting and financial control** has provided me with a deep understanding of financial statements, balance sheets, cash flows, and break-even analysis.
